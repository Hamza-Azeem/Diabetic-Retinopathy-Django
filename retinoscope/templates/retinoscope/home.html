Artificial Intelligence Impact



Diabetic retinopathy can lead to blindness if left untreated so one of the main challenges is early detection, which is crucial for successful treatment.Ophthalmologists manually analyze the fundus retinal images however, they may not always produce correct findings since even the best specialized and professional ophthalmologist may not be able to follow subtle changes in eye structure and can't detect the early stages of the disease easily also it is a very time consuming process. Therefore, automated detection for DR is necessary to solve this problem where computer-based methods and detection systems appear to offer a promising solution to support the ophthalmologists.













About RetinaScoope

We are a cutting-edge online platform dedicated to diabetic retinopathy detection. Our mission is to provide an accessible and reliable solution for early diagnosis and monitoring of this vision-threatening complication caused by diabetes. With the aim of reducing the risk of vision loss, our advanced technology combines the power of machine learning and medical expertise to analyze retinal images and detect signs of retinopathy. Through our user-friendly interface, individuals can conveniently upload their retinal images. The results are promptly generated and shared with users. At RetinaScope, we strive to make retinopathy detection efficient, and accessible to all. Join our community today and experience the benefits of early detection and intervention in preserving your vision.

RetinaScoope Mission

RetinaScoope mission is to provide a user-friendly website that enables early detection of diabetic retinopathy.
