About RetinaScope :


Welcome to RetinaScope!
We are a cutting-edge online platform dedicated to diabetic retinopathy detection.
Our mission is to provide an accessible and reliable solution for early diagnosis and monitoring of this vision-threatening complication caused by diabetes.
With the aim of reducing the risk of vision loss, our advanced technology combines the power of machine learning and medical expertise to analyze retinal images and detect signs of retinopathy with high accuracy.
Through our user-friendly interface, individuals can conveniently upload their retinal images, which are then processed using our state-of-the-art algorithms.
The results are promptly generated and shared with users, along with comprehensive reports, empowering them to take proactive steps in managing their eye health.
At RetinaScope, we strive to make retinopathy detection efficient, affordable, and accessible to all, ensuring that individuals living with diabetes can receive timely care and make informed decisions about their eye health.
